## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **CricFly TV** ("App", "we", "us", or "our"). CricFly TV is a live cricket streaming application that allows users to watch live cricket matches and sports content via video streams. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

### 2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use CricFly TV, certain technical information is automatically collected — primarily by **third-party services** integrated into the App (such as Google Firebase and Google AdMob). This information includes but is not limited to:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID), screen resolution, and hardware configuration. *(Collected by third-party analytics and advertising services.)*
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, screens viewed, buttons tapped, session duration, crashes, and errors encountered. *(Collected by third-party analytics services.)*
- **Network Information**: IP address, internet connection type (Wi-Fi or mobile data), and network availability status. *(May be collected by third-party services for ad targeting and analytics.)*
- **Performance Data**: App launch time, video buffering events, playback errors, and overall app performance metrics. *(Collected by third-party crash and performance monitoring services.)*
- **Crash Reports**: Stack traces, device state at time of crash, OS version, and app version — collected automatically by third-party crash reporting services when the app crashes or encounters an error.

### 2.2 Information You Provide

CricFly TV does **not** currently require account registration. We do not directly collect your name, email address, or password. However, any information you voluntarily provide through contact forms or support channels may be stored.

### 2.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our App integrates several third-party SDKs that collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. These are described in detail in Section 4.

## 4. Third-Party Services and SDKs

CricFly TV uses the following third-party services. Each of these may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies:

### 4.1 Google Firebase Services

We use multiple Firebase services provided by Google LLC:

#### Firebase Analytics
- **What it collects**: App events, user properties, device details, session information, and usage patterns.
- **Purpose**: To analyze App usage and improve user experience.
- **Data Retention**: 14 months (default).
- **Opt-out**: You can opt out of Firebase Analytics by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" on your device.
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

#### Firebase Crashlytics
- **What it collects**: Crash logs, stack traces, device state (OS version, device model, free RAM/disk), and app version at the time of the crash.
- **Purpose**: To identify and fix crashes and improve app stability.
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

#### Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
- **What it collects**: A device registration token (FCM token) used to deliver push notifications. No personal data is stored in the token itself.
- **Purpose**: To send you cricket match alerts, app updates, and important announcements.
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

---

### 4.2 Google AdMob

CricFly TV displays advertisements powered by **Google AdMob** (Google LLC). AdMob may collect the following:

- **Android Advertising ID (AAID)**: A resettable device identifier used for ad targeting.
- **IP Address**: Used for approximate location-based ad targeting.
- **Ad Interaction Data**: Whether you viewed or clicked an ad.
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, language, and time zone.

AdMob may use this data to show **personalized ads** based on your interests. You can control ad personalization through your device settings:
- Go to **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**
- Or reset your Advertising ID under the same menu.

- **Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **AdMob Partner Policies**: [https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

---

### 4.3 Video Streaming

CricFly TV connects to third-party CDN and streaming servers to deliver live match content. When streaming:

- The App makes **network requests** to external stream servers to fetch video content.
- These servers may log your **IP address** and **request metadata** (time, user agent, bytes transferred) as part of standard server operations.
- We do not control the data practices of third-party CDN providers hosting the streams.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- Data processed by Firebase is stored on **Google's servers**, primarily in the United States, and is subject to Google's data security and retention policies.
- Data processed by AdMob is stored on **Google's servers** and is subject to Google's advertising data policies.
- We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the App and its data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
- We do **not** operate our own servers that store your personal data.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 14 months by Firebase Analytics (configurable).
- **Crash Data**: Retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics.
- **FCM Tokens**: Active as long as the app is installed. Deleted upon app uninstall.
- **Ad Data**: Managed and retained by Google per their advertising policies.
- **Cached Images**: Stored locally on your device and cleared when you clear the App's cache or uninstall the App.
